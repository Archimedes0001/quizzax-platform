## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Welcome to Quizzax
Quizzax is your dedicated engineering assessment platform. Designed for simplicity and speed, it allows you to test your knowledge, track your progress, and compete with peers across four core engineering subjects.

### 1.2 System Requirements
- **Device**: Any smartphone, tablet, laptop, or desktop computer.
- **Browser**: Google Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Microsoft Edge (Latest versions unique recommended).
- **Internet**: Stable internet connection required.

---

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1 Accessing the Platform
1. Open your web browser.
2. Navigate to the application URL (e.g., `https://your-quiz-app.onrender.com`).
3. You will see the **Login Screen**.

### 2.2 Logging In
1. **Matric Number**: Enter your unique University Matriculation Number (e.g., `U24ICE1001`).
2. **Sign In**: Tap the "Sign In" button.
   - **Success**: You will be redirected to the Home Dashboard.
   - **Error**: If you see "Unauthorized", please check your matric number or contact the administrator.

> [!NOTE]
> You do not need a password. Your matric number is your unique identifier.

### 3.2 The Quiz Interface
Once the quiz starts:
- **Timer**: A 15-minute countdown begins immediately (top left).
- **Question**: The question text appears in the center.
- **Options**: Four multiple-choice options (A, B, C, D) are displayed.
- **Navigation**: Use "Prev" and "Next" buttons at the bottom to move between questions.

### 3.3 Answering Questions
1. **Tap an Option**: Select the answer you think is correct. It will highlight blue.
2. **Flagging**: Tap the ğŸš© flag icon (top right) to mark a question for review later.
3. **Changing Answers**: You can change your selected option at any time before submitting.

> [!TIP]
> Your progress is **auto-saved**. If you accidentally close the browser, simply log in again to resume exactly where you left off!

### 3.4 Submitting
1. When you have answered all questions, tap **"Submit Questions"** (or "Finish" on the last question).
2. A confirmation window will appear showing:
   - Number of questions answered.
   - Number of questions skipped.
3. Tap **"Submit Quiz"** to finalize your attempt.

## 4. Understanding Results

### 4.1 Result Summary
Immediately after submission, you will see your **Score Card**:
- **Total Score**: Your percentage score (e.g., 85%).
- **Stats**: Total questions, Correct answers, Incorrect answers.

### 4.2 Performance History
Tap the **"Performance"** tab (chart icon) in the bottom navigation bar to see your history:
- View your **Average Score** (CA) for each subject.
- Track your improvement over time (CA1, CA2, etc.).

### 4.3 Leaderboard
Tap the **"Leaderboard"** tab (trophy icon) to see how you rank against other students in your department and across the university.

## 5. Troubleshooting

### 5.1 Common Issues

**"I can't log in"**
- Ensure you are using the correct Matric Number format (e.g., `U24...`).
- Verify you are an authorized student for this semester.

**"The quiz froze"**
- Refresh the page. Your progress is safe and will reload automatically.

**"My score isn't showing"**
- Check your internet connection. Scores are synced to the server automatically.

### 5.2 Support
For technical support or to report a bug, please contact the IT Helpdesk at `support@quizzax.edu`.
